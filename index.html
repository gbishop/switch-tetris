Play Tetris With Switches

Configure your switch interface to use Space/Enter or Right/Left.

Choose your move with Space, Right Arrow, or by clicking the Next button on the right side of the screen.

Drop the piece with Enter, Left Arrow, or by clicking the Drop button on the left side of the screen.

The computer is offering the best and worst move on each turn. Learn to distinguish them to get a high score.

Hit any key to continue.
